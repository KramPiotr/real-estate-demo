Course Hours per Unit

Essence:
Clearly communicate the time commitment per unit on the relevant course.
The consultant should specify the number of study hours required per unit.
Mention any restrictions on the number of units that can be taken simultaneously.

Indicative phrasing
Example: "For this course, each unit requires approximately 20-24 hours of study per week. At Monash, you'll focus on one unit at a time, allowing you to fully engage with the material."
Indicative phrasing: "Let me tell you about the time commitment for this course. Each unit typically involves [X] hours of study per week. [Institution name] allows you to take [Y] units at a time, which means you'll be dedicating about [X*Y] hours weekly to your studies."
