1. Census Date Information
Definition: Consultants must explain the census date and its implications. This is crucial information for students to understand their financial and academic obligations.

Exact phrase required:

- "Census Date & You can defer or withdraw from the course without academic or financial penalty or drop a unit."

Examples of acceptable paraphrasing:

- 'The census date is the last day you can withdraw from a unit without financial or academic penalty.'
- 'You have until the census date, which is [specific date], to decide if you want to continue with the course or any specific units.'
- 'If you withdraw before the census date, you won't be charged for the unit and it won't appear on your academic record.'
