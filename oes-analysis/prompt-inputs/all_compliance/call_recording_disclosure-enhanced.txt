# Call Recording Disclosure Sub-Requirements

1. Timing of Disclosure
   - The disclosure must be made near the beginning of each call
   - It should occur within the first two minutes of the call
   - The disclosure must be made before any substantive discussion begins

2. Content of Disclosure
   - The consultant must inform the student that the call is being recorded
   - The disclosure must be stated clearly
   - Indicative phrases:
     - "Call is recorded"
     - "This call is being recorded"
