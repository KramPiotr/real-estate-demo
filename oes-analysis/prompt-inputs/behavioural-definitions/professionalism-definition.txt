<behavior_name>Professionalism and Composure</behavior_name>

<behavior_definition>
Maintaining a professional demeanor throughout the call, especially during challenging situations. This involves staying calm, focused, and respectful at all times, using appropriate language, and managing the conversation with poise and dignity.
</behavior_definition>

<behavior_essence>
Professionalism and composure are about projecting a consistent, polished image that instills confidence in the student. The consultant demonstrates this by remaining unflappable in the face of challenges, using professional language, and maintaining a courteous and respectful tone throughout the interaction. This approach helps to create a positive impression of the institution and ensures that even difficult conversations remain productive.
</behavior_essence>

<behavior_examples>
* Maintaining a calm and respectful tone even if the student becomes frustrated or angry.
* Using professional language and avoiding slang or overly casual expressions.
* Responding to challenging questions or objections with patience and respect.
* Guiding the conversation back on track if it veers off-topic, while still acknowledging the student's concerns.
* Using phrases like "I appreciate your perspective. Let me explain how we can address this concern."
* Avoiding emotional reactions and instead focusing on problem-solving: "I understand this is frustrating. Let's work together to find a solution."
</behavior_examples>
</behavior_specific>
