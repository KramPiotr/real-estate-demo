# Real Estate Objections - Extraction Definition

## Purpose
Identify, categorize, and evaluate objections raised in sales calls to build objection intelligence.

---

# PRICE OBJECTIONS

## 1.1 Asking Price Too High (`asking_price_too_high`)

### Definition
Buyer believes the listing price is higher than the property is worth.

### Detection Phrases
- "It's overpriced"
- "Other houses in the area are cheaper"
- "Not worth that much"
- "I've seen comparables for less"
- "They're asking too much"

### Resolution Approaches
- Provide recent comparables showing value justification
- Highlight unique features not present in lower-priced properties
- Discuss market conditions and trends
- Explore seller flexibility if appropriate

---

## 1.2 Budget Mismatch (`budget_mismatch`)

### Definition
Property price exceeds buyer's stated or implied budget.

### Detection Phrases
- "Over our budget"
- "More than we wanted to spend"
- "Can't afford it"
- "Out of our price range"
- "Stretching too far"

### Resolution Approaches
- Explore budget flexibility vs. hard limit
- Discuss creative financing options
- Suggest alternative properties in budget
- Help calculate true monthly cost vs. sticker shock

---

## 1.3 Value Not Justified (`value_not_justified`)

### Definition
Buyer doesn't see enough value for the price being asked.

### Detection Phrases
- "What justifies that price?"
- "Doesn't seem worth it"
- "Why so expensive?"
- "Should be cheaper given..."
- "Not enough for the money"

### Resolution Approaches
- Detail specific features and their value
- Compare to recently sold similar properties
- Highlight future value potential
- Address specific value concerns

---

# PROPERTY OBJECTIONS

## 2.1 Size/Layout Issues (`size_layout_issues`)

### Definition
Buyer has concerns about property dimensions or floor plan.

### Detection Phrases
- "Too small/big"
- "Layout doesn't work"
- "Rooms are cramped"
- "Weird floor plan"
- "Not enough space for..."
- "Kitchen is too small"
- "Need more bedrooms"

### Resolution Approaches
- Understand specific space needs
- Suggest furniture/use configurations
- Discuss renovation possibilities
- Show how previous owners used space

---

## 2.2 Condition Concerns (`condition_concerns`)

### Definition
Buyer worried about property condition, repairs, or maintenance needed.

### Detection Phrases
- "Needs too much work"
- "That roof/HVAC looks old"
- "Is that water damage?"
- "Dated/outdated"
- "Fixer-upper"
- "Maintenance issues"

### Resolution Approaches
- Provide inspection reports or disclosures
- Get estimates for concerning items
- Discuss repair credits or price adjustments
- Highlight recent updates/improvements

---

## 2.3 Location Drawbacks (`location_drawbacks`)

### Definition
Buyer objects to property location or surroundings.

### Detection Phrases
- "Too far from..."
- "Neighborhood concerns"
- "Traffic/noise"
- "Don't like the area"
- "Bad location"
- "Commute is too long"
- "Near the highway/railroad"

### Resolution Approaches
- Understand specific location priorities
- Highlight overlooked location benefits
- Provide commute time data
- Discuss neighborhood developments/improvements

---

## 2.4 Missing Features (`missing_features`)

### Definition
Property lacks features buyer considers essential.

### Detection Phrases
- "No garage/pool/basement"
- "Wish it had..."
- "Doesn't have..."
- "Need [feature] which this lacks"
- "Missing..."

### Resolution Approaches
- Clarify must-have vs. nice-to-have
- Discuss addition possibilities and costs
- Find alternative solutions (nearby gym vs. home gym)
- Show properties with desired features

---

# TIMING OBJECTIONS

## 3.1 Not Ready to Buy (`not_ready_to_buy`)

### Definition
Buyer indicates they're not prepared to make a purchase decision.

### Detection Phrases
- "Just looking"
- "Not ready yet"
- "Exploring options"
- "Too early in our search"
- "Need more time"
- "Haven't started seriously"

### Resolution Approaches
- Respect their timeline
- Understand what would make them ready
- Offer value without pressure (market updates, alerts)
- Set appropriate follow-up schedule

---

## 3.2 Waiting for Market Change (`waiting_for_market_change`)

### Definition
Buyer expects market conditions to shift in their favor.

### Detection Phrases
- "Waiting for prices to drop"
- "Market is too hot"
- "Interest rates might come down"
- "Bubble is going to burst"
- "Bad time to buy"

### Resolution Approaches
- Provide market data and expert forecasts
- Discuss cost of waiting (rent, appreciation)
- Show historical market performance
- Acknowledge uncertainty while providing perspective

---

## 3.3 Pending Life Event (`pending_life_event`)

### Definition
Buyer waiting on external event before purchasing.

### Detection Phrases
- "After we sell our house"
- "Once the divorce is final"
- "When I get the new job"
- "After the baby comes"
- "Waiting on inheritance/settlement"

### Resolution Approaches
- Understand timeline of pending event
- Offer contingency solutions
- Stay in touch with nurture campaign
- Be ready to move quickly when event occurs

---

# COMPETITION OBJECTIONS

## 4.1 Other Property Preferred (`other_property_preferred`)

### Definition
Buyer favors a competing property over the current one.

### Detection Phrases
- "We liked [address] better"
- "The other house had..."
- "Compared to what we saw..."
- "That one was nicer"
- "Prefer the other one"

### Resolution Approaches
- Understand what they preferred about competitor
- Highlight advantages competitor lacks
- Offer to show additional properties
- Know when to let go gracefully

---

## 4.2 Other Agent Relationship (`other_agent_relationship`)

### Definition
Buyer has existing relationship with another agent.

### Detection Phrases
- "Working with another agent"
- "Our agent..."
- "Already have someone"
- "Friend/family is an agent"
- "Promised to use..."

### Resolution Approaches
- Respect existing relationship
- Clarify if exclusive or casual
- Offer specific value differentiation
- Provide service even if secondary contact

---

## 4.3 Wants to Keep Looking (`wants_to_keep_looking`)

### Definition
Buyer wants to see more options before deciding.

### Detection Phrases
- "Want to see more"
- "Keep our options open"
- "Not ready to commit"
- "Explore other areas"
- "What else is there?"

### Resolution Approaches
- Understand what they're hoping to find
- Offer to show additional properties
- Set parameters for decision
- Create soft deadline based on their timeline

---

# TRUST OBJECTIONS

## 5.1 Skeptical of Agent (`skeptical_of_agent`)

### Definition
Buyer questions agent's motives, expertise, or honesty.

### Detection Phrases
- "Agents just want commission"
- "How do I know you're honest?"
- "What's in it for you?"
- "Don't trust..."
- "Pushy salespeople"

### Resolution Approaches
- Acknowledge the concern directly
- Explain fiduciary duty and process
- Provide references and reviews
- Demonstrate expertise through action

---

## 5.2 Past Bad Experience (`past_bad_experience`)

### Definition
Buyer references negative past real estate experience.

### Detection Phrases
- "Last time we bought..."
- "Our previous agent..."
- "Got burned before"
- "Bad experience with..."
- "Don't want that to happen again"

### Resolution Approaches
- Listen fully to their story
- Acknowledge what went wrong
- Explain how you're different
- Offer specific protections against repeat

---

## 5.3 Needs Third Party Validation (`needs_third_party_validation`)

### Definition
Buyer wants outside opinion before proceeding.

### Detection Phrases
- "Need to talk to my accountant/lawyer"
- "Want my parents to see it"
- "Having a friend who's an agent look"
- "Getting a second opinion"
- "Need to consult with..."

### Resolution Approaches
- Respect and encourage due diligence
- Offer to speak with their advisor
- Provide documentation for review
- Schedule follow-up after consultation

---

## Output Schema

For each objection identified:

```json
{
  "objection_id": "OBJ-001",
  "main_category": "PRICE",
  "sub_category": "budget_mismatch",
  "buyer_quote": "It's more than we wanted to spend, honestly. We were hoping to stay under $500k.",
  "transcript_range": "45-52",
  "agent_response": "I understand budget is important. Let me ask - is $500k a hard ceiling or is there flexibility if the right property came along?",
  "agent_response_range": "53-58",
  "response_effectiveness": 4,
  "effectiveness_reasoning": "Agent acknowledged concern and asked clarifying question to understand if hard limit",
  "resolution_status": "partially_resolved",
  "resolution_notes": "Buyer indicated some flexibility, discussion moved forward"
}
```

## Response Effectiveness Scoring

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 5 | Objection fully resolved, buyer more confident, moved toward commitment |
| 4 | Well handled, buyer concern reduced, conversation progressed positively |
| 3 | Adequately addressed, neutral outcome, may resurface |
| 2 | Poorly handled, buyer concern remains or increased slightly |
| 1 | Made it worse, buyer more resistant or disengaged |

## Resolution Status Values
- `resolved` - Objection fully addressed, buyer satisfied
- `partially_resolved` - Concern reduced but may return
- `unresolved` - Objection remains active
- `escalated` - Became larger issue
- `deferred` - Acknowledged, to be addressed later
