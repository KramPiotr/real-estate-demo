# Urgency Flags - Detection Definition

## Purpose
Identify time-sensitive situations requiring immediate agent action to prevent lost deals or capitalize on opportunities.

---

# CRITICAL FLAGS (Immediate Response - Within 30 Minutes)

## 1. Competitor Offer Pending (`competitor_offer_pending`)

### Definition
Buyer indicates they have or are seriously considering a competing offer, creating immediate risk of losing the deal.

### Detection Phrases
- "We have another offer"
- "The other agent said..."
- "We're also looking at [specific address/property]"
- "Another property we like"
- "We need to compare"
- "Meeting with another agent"
- "Saw something else we liked"

### Severity: CRITICAL
### Response Window: Immediate (within 30 minutes)

### Required Action
Agent must call back immediately with:
1. Acknowledge the competition without desperation
2. Reinforce unique value of current property
3. Offer to expedite any pending items
4. Discuss pre-emptive offer strategy if appropriate

### Example
> Buyer: "We really like your listing, but we saw a place yesterday on Oak Street that's also in our budget. We're meeting with that agent again tomorrow to possibly make an offer."

---

## 2. Deadline Today (`deadline_today`)

### Definition
Buyer faces a decision deadline within the current day.

### Detection Phrases
- "We need to decide today"
- "By end of day"
- "Before 5pm"
- "This evening"
- "Tonight"
- "The offer expires today"
- "They need an answer now"

### Severity: CRITICAL
### Response Window: Immediate

### Required Action
1. Clear schedule to be available
2. Prepare all necessary documentation
3. Be ready to write offer or negotiate
4. Ensure decision-makers are available

---

## 3. Viewing With Competitor (`viewing_with_competitor`)

### Definition
Buyer is seeing another property or meeting with competitor agent soon.

### Detection Phrases
- "We're seeing another place this afternoon"
- "Meeting with [agent name] later"
- "Have a showing at [time]"
- "Looking at one more today"

### Severity: CRITICAL
### Response Window: Before their competing appointment

### Required Action
1. Attempt to schedule before competitor viewing
2. Highlight unique advantages proactively
3. Create urgency around current opportunity
4. Follow up immediately after their other viewing

---

## 4. Emotional Peak (`emotional_peak`)

### Definition
Buyer expresses strong positive emotion about the property - maximum buying intent that should be captured immediately.

### Detection Phrases
- "We love it"
- "This is the one"
- "This is our house"
- "I can see us living here"
- "This feels like home"
- "We don't need to see anything else"
- "When can we make an offer?"
- "What do they need to hear?"
- "How do we do this?"

### Severity: CRITICAL
### Response Window: Immediate (strike while hot)

### Required Action
1. Acknowledge and reinforce their feelings
2. Transition to next steps immediately
3. Discuss offer strategy while enthusiasm is high
4. Schedule follow-up within 24 hours maximum
5. Don't let them leave without a commitment to next step

---

# HIGH FLAGS (Respond Within 2 Hours)

## 5. Price Objection - Serious (`price_objection_serious`)

### Definition
Buyer raises significant concern about price that could derail the deal if not addressed.

### Detection Phrases
- "It's too expensive"
- "Over our budget"
- "Can't afford it"
- "Need to think about the price"
- "More than we wanted to spend"
- "Is there any flexibility?"

### Severity: HIGH
### Response Window: Within 2 hours

### Required Action
1. Acknowledge concern without defensiveness
2. Provide market comparables
3. Discuss value justification
4. Explore creative financing options
5. Determine if budget is hard limit or preference

---

## 6. Losing Interest (`losing_interest`)

### Definition
Buyer shows signs of waning enthusiasm after previous engagement.

### Detection Phrases
- "Maybe we should keep looking"
- "Not sure anymore"
- "Need to think about it more"
- "Starting to have doubts"
- "It's fine but..."
- "We're reconsidering"

### Severity: HIGH
### Response Window: Within 2 hours

### Required Action
1. Probe to understand specific concerns
2. Address underlying issues
3. Reconnect with original motivation
4. Schedule in-person conversation if possible

---

## 7. Requested Callback (`requested_callback`)

### Definition
Buyer explicitly asked for a return call at a specific time.

### Detection Phrases
- "Can you call me back at [time]?"
- "Call me later today"
- "I'll be available at [time]"
- "Let's talk at [time]"
- "Reach me after [time]"

### Severity: HIGH
### Response Window: At or before requested time

### Required Action
1. Set reminder for exact time
2. Call precisely when promised
3. Be prepared with any information they requested

---

## 8. Inspection Concern (`inspection_concern`)

### Definition
Buyer raises concerns about property condition that could lead to deal termination.

### Detection Phrases
- "Worried about the [foundation/roof/HVAC/etc]"
- "That looks like water damage"
- "Is that mold?"
- "How old is the [system]?"
- "Will that pass inspection?"
- "We'd need to get that looked at"

### Severity: HIGH
### Response Window: Within 2 hours

### Required Action
1. Acknowledge concern seriously
2. Provide any available documentation (inspection reports, warranties)
3. Offer to get professional assessment
4. Discuss repair credits or price adjustments if appropriate

---

# MEDIUM FLAGS (Respond Within 24 Hours)

## 9. Needs More Info (`needs_more_info`)

### Definition
Buyer requested specific information or documentation.

### Detection Phrases
- "Can you send me..."
- "I'd like to see the..."
- "Do you have the [HOA docs/disclosures/reports]?"
- "What are the [taxes/HOA fees/utilities]?"
- "Can I get that in writing?"

### Severity: MEDIUM
### Response Window: Within 24 hours (same day preferred)

### Required Action
1. Gather requested information
2. Send within promised timeframe
3. Follow up to confirm receipt
4. Offer to discuss any questions

---

## 10. Wants Second Viewing (`wants_second_viewing`)

### Definition
Buyer wants to return to the property, possibly with others.

### Detection Phrases
- "Can we come back?"
- "Want to bring my [spouse/parents/contractor]"
- "Like to see it again"
- "Second look"
- "One more visit"

### Severity: MEDIUM
### Response Window: Within 24 hours (schedule promptly)

### Required Action
1. Express enthusiasm about their interest
2. Offer multiple time options
3. Prepare for questions from new attendees
4. Have relevant information ready for their concerns

---

## 11. Financing Question (`financing_question`)

### Definition
Buyer asking about payment, mortgage, or affordability details.

### Detection Phrases
- "What would our payment be?"
- "How much down?"
- "What interest rate?"
- "Can you recommend a lender?"
- "How does the mortgage process work?"

### Severity: MEDIUM
### Response Window: Within 24 hours

### Required Action
1. Provide accurate estimates
2. Connect with preferred lender if appropriate
3. Explain financing options
4. Help them understand affordability

---

## 12. Negotiation Signal (`negotiation_signal`)

### Definition
Buyer indicating interest in negotiating terms.

### Detection Phrases
- "Would they consider a lower offer?"
- "Is there any flexibility?"
- "What's their bottom line?"
- "Would they take [amount]?"
- "Are they motivated?"
- "How long has it been on market?"

### Severity: MEDIUM
### Response Window: Within 24 hours

### Required Action
1. Gauge seriousness of offer intent
2. Discuss market conditions and seller motivation
3. Help them prepare competitive offer
4. Set expectations appropriately
