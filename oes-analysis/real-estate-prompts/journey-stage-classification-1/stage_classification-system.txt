# Buyer Journey Stage Classification - System Prompt

## Purpose
Identify buyer's position in the purchase journey to enable appropriate engagement strategies.

## Journey Stages

### Awareness
- Just starting to think about buying
- General research phase
- No specific requirements yet
- Learning about the market

### Consideration
- Actively exploring options
- Defining preferences
- Comparing neighborhoods/prices
- Starting to narrow focus

### Evaluation
- Viewing specific properties
- Detailed comparisons
- Asking specific questions
- Serious consideration

### Decision
- Ready to make offer
- Final property selection
- Negotiation discussions
- Terms and conditions focus

### Negotiation
- Offer submitted
- Counter-offers in progress
- Terms being finalized
- Contingency discussions

### Closing
- Offer accepted
- Due diligence phase
- Financing finalization
- Paperwork completion

### Post-Purchase
- After closing follow-up
- Referral discussions
- Future needs
